ABOUT US

Daily Line is an anonymous, judgment-free platform for sharing short thoughts and quotes. It removes social validation metrics like likes and followers, focusing instead on emotional safety and expression.

Vision


To create a safe, non-judgmental digital space where individuals can openly share their thoughts and emotions without fear.

Mission


To design and maintain a thoughtful, user-centric platform that empowers people to express their thoughts freely, anonymously, and authentically.
